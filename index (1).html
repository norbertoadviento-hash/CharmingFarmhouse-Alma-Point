♨️ Spa Instructions

Your hot tub is controlled from the topside panel on the spa.

1 · Turn on the heater

  1. Press the “Settings” button.
  2. Press “Heat.”
  3. Set Heat Mode to “Ready.”
  4. Set Temp Range to “High.”
  5. Adjust to your desired temperature.

2 · Turn on the jets

  1. Press the “Jet” button.
  2. Select Jet 1, Jet 2, or both.

3 · When you're done

  1. Set the mode back to “Rest” and “Low.”
  2. Set the temperature to 70°F.
  3. Close the spa lid.
Please leave the spa in Rest / Low / 70°F with the lid closed when it's not in use — it protects the equipment and keeps energy costs down.
